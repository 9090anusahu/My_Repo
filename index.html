Happy Independence Day

Independence Day in India, celebrated on August 15th, marks the nation's freedom from British rule in 1947. It's a day of immense national pride, commemorating the sacrifices of freedom fighters who bravely fought for India's liberation. The Prime Minister hoists the national flag at the Red Fort in Delhi, followed by a speech addressing the nation.
